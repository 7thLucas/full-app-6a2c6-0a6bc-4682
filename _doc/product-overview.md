# Product Overview

## App Identity
- **Name**: TBD (to be confirmed by owner)
- **Working Description**: Pharmacy Inventory, Expiry & Prescription Management App
- **Tagline**: Stop expired stock. Ensure every prescription is accurate.
- **Category**: Pharmacy Operations Management

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Pharmacy owners — decision-makers with financial accountability for stock losses and compliance
- **Secondary**: Pharmacy staff / dispensers — daily operational users who log prescriptions and check stock

## Core Problem
1. **Expired drug stock**: Drugs expire unnoticed on shelves, causing direct financial losses (write-offs), regulatory violations, and patient safety risks. Manual date-tracking via paper logs or spreadsheets is unreliable and produces no early warnings.
2. **Prescription inaccuracy**: Prescriptions managed manually or inconsistently create dispensing errors, create legal compliance gaps, and leave no reliable audit trail for review or inspection.

## Solution
A focused operational tool that gives pharmacy owners and staff a single system to:
- Track all drug inventory levels, batch numbers, and stock movements
- Monitor and alert on expiry dates before they become losses
- Maintain accurate, searchable prescription records linked to drug batches
- Prevent expired stock from reaching patients via automated flags and quarantine
- Enforce prescription accuracy at the moment of dispensing

## Core Features

### 1. Drug Inventory Management
Add, update, and monitor all drug stock — name, quantity, batch number, supplier, reorder level. Full CRUD for each drug entry.

### 2. Expiry Date Tracking
Visual status flags and automated alerts at configurable thresholds (30 / 60 / 90 days before expiry). Filter and batch-manage inventory by expiry window.

### 3. Prescription Records
Log, view, and search patient prescription records. Each record is linked to the dispensed drug batch for traceability.

### 4. Expired Stock Prevention
Automatic flags quarantine near-expiry and expired batches. Expired items are surfaced prominently on the dashboard and blocked from the active dispensing queue.

### 5. Dispensing Verification
Before a dispensing event is confirmed, the system cross-checks the dispensed drug and batch against the prescription — enforcing accuracy at the point of action.

### 6. Operations Dashboard
At-a-glance view of: current stock levels, upcoming expirations (by threshold window), and recent prescription activity.

## Brand & Tone
- **Tone**: Professional, clinically precise, efficient — no unnecessary decoration
- **Visual direction**: Clean, trustworthy, medical-grade clarity; teal and white palette to signal health and safety
- **Positioning**: Purpose-built operational tool for independent and small-chain pharmacy owners who need enterprise-grade accuracy without enterprise cost or complexity

## Strategic Principles
- **Simplicity first**: Any pharmacy staff member should be productive within 10 minutes of first use
- **Accuracy over speed**: Every prescription dispensing step is confirmed before completion — no shortcuts on patient-facing actions
- **Prevention-first**: The system surfaces expiry risks before they become losses, not after — proactive over reactive

## Scope (MVP)
- Drug inventory CRUD (add, edit, remove, adjust stock)
- Expiry date tracking with configurable alert thresholds (30 / 60 / 90 days)
- Prescription record creation, linking, and lookup
- Dashboard: stock status overview, upcoming expirations, recent prescription log
- Automated expired-stock flags and quarantine logic
- Dispensing verification step before prescription confirmation
